stage = theater & Vita = life so stageVita is your Stage life

The Theatrical resume is the tool you use to obtain roles, lighting gigs, director roles. The problem you face is the resume is in constant flux new roles are added, out dated ones are removed, your photo needs to change as they mature. Currently most theater people have this info scattered all over in paper and as data on a variety of systems.

Why StageVita

Update your theatrical resume anytime and share it in minutes.

Should you choose to, have your theatrical resume always available for review by directors and theater companies.
No need to search for your resume in a cluttered hard drive.

No need to print copies of your resume to take to every audition.(although this option is still available).

No need to provide biographical information to the Playbill Manager for each of your show's playbills.

Your current resume is not only at your fingertips, but also - should you choose to - at the fingertips of directors and theater companies searching for actors and tech staff.
